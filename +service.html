We provide modern design services for small and medium-sized businesses. Our logos, brandbooks, presentations and visualizations have been used by companies for years to attract new customers, position themselves securely in the market, and look professional.
What sets us apart from other similar companies on the market? If others do a good design - we're likely to be cheaper. If someone else charges as much as we do - we are likely to have better quality. We don't offer a unique service, we offer simple good modern design that will last for years.
We are not shy about showing our work. We have transparent and honest pricing: we don't write that a design costs from $100 when we know it will cost $1,000. And we strive to provide our clients with designs that we ourselves will enjoy first and foremost.... yes, we have a sense of beauty:)
And we're easy to do business with because we only work with clients who share our values.
Our services
1. Presentations

We develop presentations. They are well structured and modern looking. Period.

Usually we are ordered both redesign of existing layouts and design "from scratch". If your company does not have a corporate identity - we can make a template with basic elements (colors, fonts, basic elements) as part of the work on the presentation. If necessary, we will also draw infographics, custom icons, graphics and anything else you need.

2. Prints

Sometimes it's easier to show a customer a brochure-style presentation than to open a computer. Plus, many people like products that they can touch. Travel agencies, construction companies, startups, government agencies - they all use printed products to promote their services. If you're in Calgary - we can recommend a great place to print.

3. Visualization and 3d modeling

Sometimes it is necessary to make a visualization of the goods, location, process diagram to present their services. Especially often this service is used by builders who need to sell real estate, but they only have drawings in hand.

We help with both visualization of interiors and 3d modeling of almost any objects.

A company's logo is where the perception of the company begins. Having a stylish, modern logo is a must for businesses that care about their brand.

We can help you both with the logo separately and with the development of a complete brand book (colors, fonts, elements, etc.).

Work process
1
Briefing

This initial step involves filling out a comprehensive brief that outlines the project requirements and establishing a clear understanding of the work scope. We also agree upon the associated cost for the project.

[Contract signing, 50% advanced payment]

2
Evaluate work, sign contract/NDA

In this phase, we collaborate with the client to obtain all necessary materials and information required for the presentation. This includes relevant content, images, and any specific guidelines or preferences.

3
Inputs and calculations

Our team proceeds with the development of the presentation layout, adhering to the client's wishes and specifications. We ensure that the design reflects their branding and style preferences while effectively conveying the intended message.

4
Provide general dashboard design

Once the initial layout is ready, we present it to the client for approval. Any necessary adjustments or modifications are made based on the client's feedback and preferences, ensuring their satisfaction with the overall design.

5
Putting into operations

We meticulously refine and finalize the presentation, taking care of all the requested revisions. Our team ensures that the project is completed to the highest standards and meets the client's expectations. Once satisfied, the client provides final approval.

6
Documentation and
support

In the last step, we transfer all relevant sources, including the PowerPoint file, as agreed upon with the client. This allows them to access and utilize the presentation in their preferred manner.

[Work completion signing, 50% final payment]
