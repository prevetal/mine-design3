The most popular button in every ERP system is ”export to Excel”. All modern finance world is working with calculations in Excel. All consultants for Big-3 and Big-4 use Excel as their primary tool for all sorts of calculations: forecasts, plans, development new methodologies, etc. Even when you’d like to add new functionality to your enterprise system, you will do all the logic in Excel first, then calculate economic viability of the idea for investment committee in the same Excel.

There is a reason why Excel is so popular in business world - it’s versatile. It’s almost limitless with its capabilities. With Powerpivot for database processing, VBA for automation, VSTO/.net for native applications - Excel is capable to do whatever you want, fast and easy to understand. It looks simple but it’s better to know all functionality to choose the proper solutions for every task.
